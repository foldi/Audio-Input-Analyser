Audio Input Analyser

Use the Audio Input Analyser to receive real-time audio analysis of your computer microphone's audio stream. The script uses both the getUserMedia and Web Audio APIs and is compatible in the latest version of Chrome. Other browsers may catch up soon.

Simple

A bare bones implementation of the Audio Input Analyser. Simply subscribe to the 'meter' event and receive data in your callback.

Band10

An example of a 10 band equalizer plus average volume meter.

Band31

An example of a 31 band equalizer plus average volume meter.

Flora example

Creates flocking agents that are attracted to both 1000hz and 2500Hz tones. Play a tone generator near your microphone to see the effect.
